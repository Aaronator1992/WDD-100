Uber: Above and Beyond

The company that I worked for which is a shared ride networking company that uses an app that requests, not hails an Uber driver.
the payment system works, so that a person only uses the electronic app instead of cash. however, the person can chose to tip the driver based on how the driver interacted with the person.

There is a two way rating system in the app that provides intel to the uber company on how the driver is interacting with the passenger and the passenger with the driver.
both have a level of expectation, and if the person driving acts a little weird, the passenger notices and rates accordingly.
I learned that talking to the passenger does help with the rating.

Uber rating system

  • 5 star rating system
  • Both the driver and the rider rate each other
  • sometimes the rider tips the driver
